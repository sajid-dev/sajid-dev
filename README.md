## About

Senior full stack engineer with 11 years of experience shipping production software across web, mobile, and backend. I specialise in **React** and **React Native** on the frontend, **Node.js** and **Python** on the backend, and have spent the last two years building AI features powered by **RAG pipelines** — document ingestion, vector search, and LLM-generated responses integrated into real products.

I work best in small, high-ownership teams where engineers drive decisions. I've taken features from architecture through to production, led technical decisions, and consistently shipped across the full stack without handing off between layers.

Looking for **senior full stack** or **founding engineer** roles — startups, scale-ups, and established product teams.
